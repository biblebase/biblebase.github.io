聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB罗波安就立她所生的儿子亚比雅为太子，以便让他继承王位。
CNV於是羅波安立了瑪迦的兒子亞比雅作太子，在他的兄弟中為首，因為羅波安想立他繼承王位。
CUNP羅波安立瑪迦的兒子亞比雅作太子，在他弟兄中為首，因為想要立他接續作王。
NASBRehoboam appointed Abijah the son of Maacah as head and leader among his brothers, for he intended to make him king.
NIVRehoboam appointed Abijah son of Maakah as crown prince among his brothers, in order to make him king.
NLTRehoboam appointed Maacah’s son Abijah as leader among the princes, making it clear that he would be the next king.
