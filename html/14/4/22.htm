CCB蜡剪、碗、碟和火鼎。殿门和至圣所的门也是金的。
CNV又有剪刀、盤子、調羹和火鼎，都是用精金做的。殿門，就是進入至聖所裡面的門和正殿的門，都是金的。
CUNP又用精金製造鑷子、盤子、調羹、火鼎。至於殿門和至聖所的門扇，並殿的門扇，都是金子妝飾的。
NASBand the snuffers, the bowls, the spoons and the firepans of pure gold; and the entrance of the house, its inner doors for the holy of holies and the doors of the house, that is, of the nave, of gold.
NIVthe pure gold wick trimmers, sprinkling bowls, dishes and censers; and the gold doors of the temple: the inner doors to the Most Holy Place and the doors of the main hall.
NLTthe lamp snuffers, bowls, ladles, and incense burners—all of solid gold; the doors for the entrances to the Most Holy Place and the main room of the Temple, overlaid with gold.
