CNV你們不要像你們的列祖和你們的親族，他們得罪了耶和華他們列祖的　神，以致耶和華使他們成為令人驚駭的對象，就像你們自己所見的。

NIVDo not be like your parents and your fellow Israelites, who were unfaithful to the Lord, the God of their ancestors, so that he made them an object of horror, as you see.
NLTDo not be like your ancestors and relatives who abandoned the Lord, the God of their ancestors, and became an object of derision, as you yourselves can see.
