CCB书中记述了他的政绩和英勇事迹，以及他和以色列及列国所遭遇的事。

CUNP他的國事和他的勇力，以及他和以色列並列國所經過的事都寫在這書上。
NASBwith all his reign, his power, and the circumstances which came on him, on Israel, and on all the kingdoms of the lands.
NIVtogether with the details of his reign and power, and the circumstances that surrounded him and Israel and the kingdoms of all the other lands.
NLTThese accounts include the mighty deeds of his reign and everything that happened to him and to Israel and to all the surrounding kingdoms.
