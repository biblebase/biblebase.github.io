CUNP我們在你面前是客旅，是寄居的，與我們列祖一樣。我們在世的日子如影兒，不能長存。

NLTWe are here for only a moment, visitors and strangers in the land as our ancestors were before us. Our days on earth are like a passing shadow, gone so soon without a trace.
