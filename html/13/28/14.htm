CCB制造各种金器银器所需金银的分量——
CNV大衛又指示了他用多少金子做各樣需用的金器；用多少銀子做各樣需用的銀器；
CUNP以及各樣應用金器的分兩和各樣應用銀器的分兩，
NASBfor the golden utensils, the weight of gold for all utensils for every kind of service; for the silver utensils, the weight of silver for all utensils for every kind of service;
NIVHe designated the weight of gold for all the gold articles to be used in various kinds of service, and the weight of silver for all the silver articles to be used in various kinds of service:
NLTDavid gave instructions regarding how much gold and silver should be used to make the items needed for service.
