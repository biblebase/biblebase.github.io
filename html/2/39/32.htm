聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB圣幕，就是会幕的工作就这样全部完成了，都是以色列人照耶和华对摩西的吩咐做的。
CNV於是，帳幕，就是會幕的一切工程都完成了；凡是耶和華吩咐摩西的，以色列人都照著作了；這樣，他們就作完了會幕的一切工程。
CUNP帳幕，就是會幕，一切的工就這樣做完了。凡耶和華所吩咐摩西的，以色列人都照樣做了。
NASBThus all the work of the tabernacle of the tent of meeting was completed; and the sons of Israel did according to all that the Lord had commanded Moses; so they did.
NIVSo all the work on the tabernacle, the tent of meeting, was completed. The Israelites did everything just as the Lord commanded Moses.
NLTAnd so at last the Tabernacle was finished. The Israelites had done everything just as the Lord had commanded Moses.
