NASBThe incense which you shall make, you shall not make in the same proportions for yourselves; it shall be holy to you for the Lord.
NIVDo not make any incense with this formula for yourselves; consider it holy to the Lord.
NLTNever use this formula to make this incense for yourselves. It is reserved for the Lord, and you must treat it as holy.
