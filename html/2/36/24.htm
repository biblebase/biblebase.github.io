CNV在這二十塊板底下，做了四十個銀座；這塊板底下的兩個銀座用來連接板的兩個榫頭，那塊板底下的兩個銀座用來連接它的兩個榫頭。
CUNP在這二十塊板底下又做四十個帶卯的銀座：兩卯接這塊板上的兩榫，兩卯接那塊板上的兩榫。
NASBand he made forty sockets of silver under the twenty boards; two sockets under one board for its two tenons and two sockets under another board for its two tenons.
NIVand made forty silver bases to go under them—two bases for each frame, one under each projection.
NLTHe also made forty silver bases—two bases under each frame, with the pegs fitting securely into the bases.
