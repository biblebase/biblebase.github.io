聖經版本

CCB然后在每幅大幔子边缘钉上了蓝色的扣环，
CNV在第一組相連的幔子末幅的邊緣上，他做藍色的鈕扣；在另一組相連的幔子末幅的邊緣上，他也照樣作。
CUNP在這相連的幔子末幅邊上做藍色的鈕扣，在那相連的幔子末幅邊上也照樣做；
NASBHe made loops of blue on the edge of the outermost curtain in the first set; he did likewise on the edge of the curtain that was outermost in the second set.
NIVThen they made loops of blue material along the edge of the end curtain in one set, and the same was done with the end curtain in the other set.
NLTHe made fifty loops of blue yarn and put them along the edge of the last curtain in each set.
