聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB但王要因上帝而欢欣， 凡信靠祂的人必欢喜快乐， 说谎者的口必被封住。
CNV但王必因　神歡喜； 所有指著　神起誓的，都必可以誇耀； 說謊的人的口卻必被塞住。
CUNP但是王必因神歡喜。 凡指着他發誓的必要誇口， 因為說謊之人的口必被塞住。
NASBBut the king will rejoice in God; Everyone who swears by Him will glory, For the mouths of those who speak lies will be stopped.
NIVBut the king will rejoice in God; all who swear by God will glory in him, while the mouths of liars will be silenced.
NLTBut the king will rejoice in God. All who swear to tell the truth will praise him, while liars will be silenced.
