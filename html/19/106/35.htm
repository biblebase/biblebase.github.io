聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB反倒与他们混居， 仿效他们的习俗，
CNV反而和外族人混居雜處， 學習他們的風俗習慣。
CUNP反與他們混雜相合， 學習他們的行為，
NASBBut they mingled with the nations And learned their practices,
NIVbut they mingled with the nations and adopted their customs.
NLTInstead, they mingled among the pagans and adopted their evil customs.
