CNV而是由　神來決定； 他使這人降卑，使那人升高。
CUNP惟有神斷定； 他使這人降卑，使那人升高。
NASBBut God is the Judge; He puts down one and exalts another.
NIVIt is God who judges: He brings one down, he exalts another.
NLTIt is God alone who judges; he decides who will rise and who will fall.
