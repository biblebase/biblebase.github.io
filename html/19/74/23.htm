CNV求你不要忘記你敵人的聲音， 不要忘記那些起來與你為敵的人不住加劇地喧鬧。
CUNP不要忘記你敵人的聲音； 那起來敵你之人的喧嘩時常上升。
NASBDo not forget the voice of Your adversaries, The uproar of those who rise against You which ascends continually.
NIVDo not ignore the clamor of your adversaries, the uproar of your enemies, which rises continually.
NLTDon’t overlook what your enemies have said or their growing uproar.
