聖經版本

CCB因为祂没有忘记祂赐给仆人亚伯拉罕的神圣应许。
CNV因為他記念他向自己的僕人亞伯拉罕應許的聖言。
CUNP這都因他記念他的聖言 和他的僕人亞伯拉罕。
NASBFor He remembered His holy word With Abraham His servant;
NIVFor he remembered his holy promise given to his servant Abraham.
NLTFor he remembered his sacred promise to his servant Abraham.
