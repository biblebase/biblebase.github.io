聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB他们不是靠自己的刀剑征服那里， 不是靠自己的臂膀得胜， 而是靠你的权能、力量和恩惠， 因为你爱他们。
CNV因為他們取得那地，不是靠自己的刀劍； 他們得勝，也不是靠自己的膀臂； 而是靠你的右手、你的膀臂和你臉上的光， 因為你喜悅他們。
CUNP因為他們不是靠自己的刀劍得地土， 也不是靠自己的膀臂得勝， 乃是靠你的右手、你的膀臂， 和你臉上的亮光， 因為你喜悅他們。
NASBFor by their own sword they did not possess the land, And their own arm did not save them, But Your right hand and Your arm and the light of Your presence, For You favored them.
NIVIt was not by their sword that they won the land, nor did their arm bring them victory; it was your right hand, your arm, and the light of your face, for you loved them.
NLTThey did not conquer the land with their swords; it was not their own strong arm that gave them victory. It was your right hand and strong arm and the blinding light from your face that helped them, for you loved them.
