聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB就是那些守祂的约、 一心遵行祂命令的人。
CNV就是那些謹守他的約， 記住他的訓詞，並且遵行的人。
CUNP就是那些遵守他的約、 記念他的訓詞而遵行的人。
NASBTo those who keep His covenant And remember His precepts to do them.
NIVwith those who keep his covenant and remember to obey his precepts.
NLTof those who are faithful to his covenant, of those who obey his commandments!
