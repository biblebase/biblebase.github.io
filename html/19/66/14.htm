聖經版本

CCB就是我在危难中许下的誓言。
CNV就是在急難的時候， 我嘴唇所許的，我口裡所說的。
CUNP就是在急難時我嘴唇所發的、 口中所許的。
NASBWhich my lips uttered And my mouth spoke when I was in distress.
NIVvows my lips promised and my mouth spoke when I was in trouble.
NLTyes, the sacred vows that I made when I was in deep trouble.
