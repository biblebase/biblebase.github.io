CCB我们不要在子孙面前隐瞒事实， 要把耶和华当受称颂的作为、 祂的权能和所行的奇事告诉下一代。
CNV我們不把這些事向他們的子孫隱瞞， 卻要把耶和華應得的讚美、他的能力， 和他所行的奇事， 都向後代的人述說。
CUNP我們不將這些事向他們的子孫隱瞞， 要將耶和華的美德和他的能力， 並他奇妙的作為，述說給後代聽。
NASBWe will not conceal them from their children, But tell to the generation to come the praises of the Lord, And His strength and His wondrous works that He has done.
NIVWe will not hide them from their descendants; we will tell the next generation the praiseworthy deeds of the Lord, his power, and the wonders he has done.
NLTWe will not hide these truths from our children; we will tell the next generation about the glorious deeds of the Lord, about his power and his mighty wonders.
