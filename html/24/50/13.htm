CNV因耶和華的烈怒，巴比倫必沒有人居住， 完全荒廢； 每一個住過巴比倫的， 都必因這地遭受的一切創傷而驚駭， 並且嗤笑它。

NIVBecause of the Lord’s anger she will not be inhabited but will be completely desolate. All who pass Babylon will be appalled; they will scoff because of all her wounds.
NLTBecause of the Lord’s anger, Babylon will become a deserted wasteland. All who pass by will be horrified and will gasp at the destruction they see there.
