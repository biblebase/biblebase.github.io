NIVThese were the clans of Ephraim; those numbered were 32,500. These were the descendants of Joseph by their clans.
NLTThese were the clans of Ephraim. Their registered troops numbered 32,500. These clans of Manasseh and Ephraim were all descendants of Joseph.
