CCB利亚说：“我把婢女送给丈夫做妾，现在上帝给我报酬了。”因此，她就为孩子取名叫以萨迦。
CNV利亞說：“　神給了我酬報，因為我把我的婢女給了我的丈夫。”於是給孩子起名叫以薩迦。
CUNP利亞說：「神給了我價值，因為我把使女給了我丈夫」，於是給他起名叫以薩迦。
NASBThen Leah said, “God has given me my wages because I gave my maid to my husband.” So she named him Issachar.
NIVThen Leah said, “God has rewarded me for giving my servant to my husband.” So she named him Issachar.
NLTShe named him Issachar, for she said, “God has rewarded me for giving my servant to my husband as a wife.”
