NASBSow your seed in the morning and do not be idle in the evening, for you do not know whether morning or evening sowing will succeed, or whether both of them alike will be good.
NIVSow your seed in the morning, and at evening let your hands not be idle, for you do not know which will succeed, whether this or that, or whether both will do equally well.
NLTPlant your seed in the morning and keep busy all afternoon, for you don’t know if profit will come from one activity or another—or maybe both.
