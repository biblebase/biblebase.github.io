聖經版本

中英文各選取三個常用版本。
CCB所以，你要抛开心中的烦恼和肉体的痛苦，因为青春年华转瞬即逝。
CNV所以你當除掉心中的煩惱，除去肉體的疾苦，因為無論是幼年或是壯年，都是虛空的。
CUNP所以，你當從心中除掉愁煩，從肉體克去邪惡；因為一生的開端和幼年之時，都是虛空的。
NASBSo, remove grief and anger from your heart and put away pain from your body, because childhood and the prime of life are fleeting.
NIVSo then, banish anxiety from your heart and cast off the troubles of your body, for youth and vigor are meaningless.
NLTSo refuse to worry, and keep your body healthy. But remember that youth, with a whole life before you, is meaningless.
