CCB对受教者而言， 智者的责备犹如金耳环和金饰物。
CNV智慧人的責備在聽從的人耳中， 好像金耳環和精金的飾物。
CUNP智慧人的勸戒，在順從的人耳中， 好像金耳環和精金的妝飾。
NASBLike an earring of gold and an ornament of fine gold Is a wise reprover to a listening ear.
NIVLike an earring of gold or an ornament of fine gold is the rebuke of a wise judge to a listening ear.
NLTTo one who listens, valid criticism is like a gold earring or other gold jewelry.
