NASBWhen the scoffer is punished, the naive becomes wise; But when the wise is instructed, he receives knowledge.
NIVWhen a mocker is punished, the simple gain wisdom; by paying attention to the wise they get knowledge.
NLTIf you punish a mocker, the simpleminded become wise; if you instruct the wise, they will be all the wiser.
