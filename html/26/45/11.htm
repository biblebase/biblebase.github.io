CCB贺梅珥是标准的容量单位，量固体的伊法是十分之一贺梅珥，量液体的罢特也是十分之一贺梅珥。
CNV量固體的‘伊法’和量液體的‘罷特’的容量要相同：一‘罷特’等於‘賀梅珥’的十分之一；一‘伊法’也等於‘賀梅珥’的十分之一，都以‘賀梅珥’的容量為準。
CUNP伊法與罷特大小要一樣。罷特可盛賀梅珥十分之一，伊法也可盛賀梅珥十分之一，都以賀梅珥的大小為準。
NASBThe ephah and the bath shall be the same quantity, so that the bath will contain a tenth of a homer and the ephah a tenth of a homer; their standard shall be according to the homer.
NIVThe ephah and the bath are to be the same size, the bath containing a tenth of a homer and the ephah a tenth of a homer; the homer is to be the standard measure for both.
NLTThe homer will be your standard unit for measuring volume. The ephah and the bath will each measure one-tenth of a homer.
