NASBMoreover, the word of the Lord through the prophet Jehu the son of Hanani also came against Baasha and his household, both because of all the evil which he did in the sight of the Lord, provoking Him to anger with the work of his hands, in being like the house of Jeroboam, and because he struck it.
NIVMoreover, the word of the Lord came through the prophet Jehu son of Hanani to Baasha and his house, because of all the evil he had done in the eyes of the Lord, arousing his anger by the things he did, becoming like the house of Jeroboam—and also because he destroyed it.
NLTThe message from the Lord against Baasha and his family came through the prophet Jehu son of Hanani. It was delivered because Baasha had done what was evil in the Lord’s sight (just as the family of Jeroboam had done), and also because Baasha had destroyed the family of Jeroboam. The Lord’s anger was provoked by Baasha’s sins.

解經

豐盛生命研讀本

16:7

約沙法：約沙法是個好君王。他致力於教導國民遵從神的道，並且立定心意尋求神(關於約沙法統治,參22:41~50；代下17:1~21:1)。

雷厲研讀本聖經

雷厲

一六7"他殺了耶羅波安的全家"。雖然神從前定意要使耶羅波安的王朝遭毀滅（一四10～14），但巴沙仍要為這惡行而負責任。
