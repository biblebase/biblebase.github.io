CUNP這樣，米甸人被以色列人制伏了，不敢再擡頭。基甸還在的日子，國中太平四十年。

NIVThus Midian was subdued before the Israelites and did not raise its head again. During Gideon’s lifetime, the land had peace forty years.
NLTThat is the story of how the people of Israel defeated Midian, which never recovered. Throughout the rest of Gideon’s lifetime—about forty years—there was peace in the land.
